BEAM Method

Each source is labeled using the BEAM framework — a way of categorizing how a source functions in your research, rather than just what it says.

B

Background — establishes context, history, or definitions

E

Exhibit — a text, case, or example you analyze directly

A

Argument — a source whose claim you engage, extend, or push back on

M

Method — provides a framework or approach you apply to your own analysis
