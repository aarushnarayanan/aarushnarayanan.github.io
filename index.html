The Argument

A helpful tool ‚Äî or a very convincing
performance of one?

Agentic AI ‚Äî systems that don't just answer but act ‚Äî is being woven into the digital spaces where we live: our inboxes, calendars, files, and browsers. The common understanding is that these tools serve us. But whose goals they optimize for, whose interests they were built to advance, and what they do with the data they generate along the way ‚Äî these are questions that sit below the waterline.

This project asks not whether agentic AI is good or bad, but why it exists, who benefits from it, and what ordinary people risk losing inside a space they share with a system they didn't design.
